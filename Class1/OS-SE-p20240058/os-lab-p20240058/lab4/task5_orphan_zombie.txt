--- Zombie and Orphan Cleanup Notes ---
Q: How are orphans cleaned up? A: They are adopted by init (PID 1) which calls wait() when they exit.
Q: How are zombies cleaned up? A: When the parent finally calls wait(), or when the parent itself exits and init inherits and cleans them.
Q: Can you kill a zombie with 'kill -9'? A: No. A zombie is already dead — it has no running code. Only the parent calling wait() cleans it.
